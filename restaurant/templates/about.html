About Us

Based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, Little Lemon is a family owned Indian restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from authentic Indian culture and have a menu of 12-13 items.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Little Lemon is owned by two Indian brothers, Aman & Chaman, who did Hotel Management to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, Aman relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Mumbai.

Chaman does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Indian to incorporate additional cuisines.
